Our Vision & Mission

CSMA Vision is to establish this exclusive forum of the CBSE Schools Management for the schools functioning across the world and exchange all innovative ideas in enhancing the school's performance, assist in day to day issue, acting as a bridge to address all regulatory demands keeping in mind the welfare of the student community as the bottom line.

As a first phase, CSMA will work towards establishing its executive member council across the state of Tamilnadu categorizing to the districts and zone levels. Every district / zone will be created based on the members and the geography and appropriate district wise or zone wise representation will be formed.

CSMA 's set milestone of making this as a global forum will bring together all the state level forums of CBSE Schools management  It create a global federation of CBSE Schools management which will have state wide representation as well
